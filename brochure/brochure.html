About the Programme

Transforming Healthcare Through Prevention

The National Preventive Medicine Residency Programme encompasses the previous national specialist training programmes in Public Health and Occupational Medicine.

Programme Director

We are an internationally recognised training centre that has produced leaders in Public Health and Occupational Medicine in Singapore, regionally and globally for more than five decades.

Mission-Driven

Focus on the health of individuals and communities to promote, protect and maintain health and well-being while preventing disease, disability and premature death.

• Core rotations in primary care, infectious disease, and policy

Admission Requirements

Join Our Elite Programme

Based on the latest residency requirements from MOH Holdings and NUHS

Basic Requirements

  • Completed Post-Graduate Year 1 (PGY1) by commencement
  • Employment offer from MOH Holdings
  • Medical degree registrable with Singapore Medical Council
  • Full or Conditional SMC Registration

Application Process

  • Apply through ERAS system
  • 3-4 referee letters (minimum 3 weeks supervision)
  • Programme-specific interviews
  • Rank preferences after interviews

Career Requirements

  • Mandatory Master of Public Health degree
  • First-author peer-reviewed publication
  • Pass Exit Examination
  • Complete specialist accreditation requirements
