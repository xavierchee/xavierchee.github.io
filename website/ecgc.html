Executive and Graduate Certificates (ECGC)

Our Precision Medicine programme offers unparalleled flexibility through two learning paths:

  • Executive Certificates (EC): Take individual modules as standalone courses to build targeted expertise.
  • Graduate Certificates (GC): Bundle two related modules into a comprehensive credential that provides deeper specialization.

Certificate to Master's Conversion: Building Your Precision Medicine Degree

Earn your Master's in Precision Medicine step by step through our Executive Certificates (ECs) and Graduate Certificates (GCs). Working professionals can complete these certificates at their own pace.

Later, when enrolling in the full Master's programme, any qualifying certificates completed with a minimum Cumulative Average Point (CAP) of 3.5 can count toward degree requirements.

Please note that ECs and GCs are valid for credit conversion for up to 5 years from completion date, ensuring your knowledge remains current in this rapidly evolving field.

Note: Some core modules in the Master's programme are exclusively available to enrolled Master's students, including PHM5004 – High Performance Computing for Precision Medicine, PHM5005 – AI and Machine Learning for Precision Medicine and PHM5007 – Seminars in Precision Medicine.
